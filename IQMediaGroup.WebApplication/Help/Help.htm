If you are facing problem in order to make a clip then please follow the steps below depending on your browser.After applying following steps you need to restart your browser.

Internet Explorer
1. Click on Tools -> Internet Options
2. Go to Privacy Policy
3. Click on Advance button.
4. If Override automatic cookie handling checkbox is checked then under "Third-party cookies", Choose "Accept" radio button
5. If Override automatic cookie handling checkbox is not checked then choose "Low" settings from select slider.

Mozila Firefox
1. Click Tools -> Options.
2. Go to Privacy tab.
3. Check "Accept third party cookies" checkbox.

Google Chrome
1. Click Tools Options.
2. Go to Under the hood tab.
3. Go to Privacy -> Cookie settings.
4. From dropdown box choose "Allow all cookies"

Safari
1. Go to Tools -> Preference
2. Go to Security tab.
3. Under the Accept cookies choose option "Always"
